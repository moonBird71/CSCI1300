About Me

My name is Sonia. I am a freshman at the University of Nebraska at Omaha (UNO) . I am a returning adult learner trying to earn a second BA - in Info Assurance. I already have a BA in English and work as a retail clerk at a chain store. I hope that UNO will lead me to better prospects in the future.

I am an anime and RPG fan. I used to watch subbed anime online, read translated scans online, and go to conventions. Now I restrain myself to watching other people playthrough RPGs on Youtube and reading fanfiction. This is due to how distracted I would let myself get and how little work I would get done.

My favorite RPGs are the Final Fantasy series, though I have a soft spot for the nostalgia of Pokemon. My favorite anime tends toward the supernatural genre, with some romantic and slice of life. Though I often enjoy fanfictions of those games and animes more than the original works. Particularly fanfics that explore character development and world building - which are often lacking, given the medium.



Education

I have already earned an English BA and a minor in Business Administration, and am currently an undecided major in UNO's College of IS&T. Though undecided at the moment I am leaning toward earning a BS in Information Assurance (IA) and hope to gain pratical experience in the field while still in school.





Recreation

In my free time, I read fanfiction online - I have favorite authors and favorite source materials that I usually check first for updates. Right now I am reading fanfics about the Korean Manhwa "The Gamer" and before that I perused the Japanese manga "Katekyo Hitman Reborn."

When I'm not reading fanfics, I enjoy going onto YouTube and watching RPG playthroughs. Usually I watch Final Fantasy playthroughs, but sometimes I watch the playthrough of a game series that I hear about to see if I like it and it's related works. I did that for playthroughs of "Vampire: The Masquerade" and "Mass Effect."
